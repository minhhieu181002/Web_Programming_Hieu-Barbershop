<p>The undercut hairstyle is a popular choice known for its versatility and modern appeal. Whether you're going for a subtle or bold look, mastering the undercut requires precision and attention to detail. </p>

<h4>Step 1: Gather the Necessary Tools </h4>
  <ul>
    <li>Clippers with various guard sizes</li>
    <li>Comb and hair clips</li>
    <li>Scissors</li>
    <li>Mirror</li>
    <li>Towel and hair cape</li>
  </ul>
  <img class="rounded" src="/babershop design/blog img/dung cu hot toc.jpg" alt="Undercut Hairstyle Image" ">

<h4>Step 2: Preparation</h4>
<ul>
<li>Wash and dry the hair thoroughly.</li>
<li>Section off the top part of the hair that will be left longer.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Step 3: Clipper Work on the Sides and Back</h4>
<ul>
<img class="rounded" src="/babershop design/blog img/undercut_side1_minh.jpg" alt="Undercut Hairstyle Image" ">
<p>Start with the desired guard size (e.g., #3) and work upwards for a gradual fade. </p>
 <li>Use the clippers against the grain for a cleaner cut.</li>
<li>Define the hairline and neckline for a clean finish.</li>
</ul>
<img class="rounded" src="/babershop design/blog img/undercut_side2_minh.jpg" alt="Undercut Hairstyle Image" ">

<h4>Step 4: Blending and Fading</h4>

<li>Change guard sizes gradually (e.g., #2 to #1) to create a smooth transition.</li>
<li>Blend the shorter sides with the longer top, using a comb and scissors for precision.</li>
<img class="rounded" src="/babershop design/blog img/undercut_gay_minh.jpg" alt="Undercut Hairstyle Image" ">
<h4>Step 5: Styling the Top</h4>
<ul>
<li>Trim the top to the desired length and style it as per preference.</li>
</li>Apply styling products (wax, pomade) for hold and texture.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Step 6: Final Touches</h4>
<ul>
<li>Clean the neckline and edges for a polished look.</li>
<li>Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots and make necessary adjustments.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Conclusion</h4>
<p>Cutting an undercut requires attention to detail and patience. With practice and the right technique, achieving a professional-looking undercut hairstyle is possible.</p>
